Integrated Curriculum

Our comprehensive 10-week program designed to foster creativity and innovation.

Foundation & Culture (Weeks 1-4)

Week 1
Creativity & Economy

Focus: Introducing creativity as a key economic driver.

Output: Personal creativity statement.

Week 2
Design Thinking

Focus: Foundations of design thinking.

Output: Redesigning a concept.

Week 3
Visual Literacy

Focus: Visual literacy and creative communication.

Output: Branded poster or digital graphics.

Week 4
Culture & Art

Focus: Rwandan culture and contemporary art.

Output: Modernized cultural designs.

Production & Entrepreneurship (Weeks 5-8)

Week 5
Digital Production

Focus: Digital production basics.

Output: 30–60 seconds edited video.

Week 6
Performing Arts

Focus: Performing arts techniques.

Output: 1–2 minutes performance.

Week 7
Entrepreneurship

Focus: Creative entrepreneurship fundamentals.

Output: Mini business model canvas.

Week 8
Product Development

Focus: Creative product development.

Output: First product prototype.

Refinement & Showcase (Weeks 9-10)

Week 9
Rehearsals

Focus: Rehearsals and mock presentations.

Output: Polished final product.

Week 10
Final Showcase

Focus: Showcase.

Output: End-of-term showcase.
